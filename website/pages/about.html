Built on a Simple Belief

Everyone deserves fiduciary financial advice—advice that puts your interests first, available whenever you need it, at a price that doesn’t erode your wealth.

Our Mission

Meridian Wealth Advisors was founded to remove the barriers that keep quality financial advice out of reach. Traditional advisory firms charge high fees, operate on limited schedules, and often carry conflicts of interest that work against clients. We believe technology can solve every one of those problems.

By combining advanced AI systems with rigorous compliance standards and a fiduciary-first culture, we deliver institutional-quality wealth management to individuals and families—at fees 20–40% below the industry average.

Our Core Values

Fiduciary First

Every recommendation we make is legally required to be in your best interest. No commissions, no revenue sharing, no product sales. Your goals are our only motivation.

Radical Transparency

We publish our fees, explain our AI technology openly, disclose every potential conflict of interest, and document the reasoning behind every piece of advice we provide.

Relentless Consistency

AI doesn’t have bad days, biases toward certain products, or incentives to churn your account. You receive the same disciplined, evidence-based approach every single time.

Always Available

Markets don’t sleep, and neither does your financial life. Our AI advisors are available 24/7 via chat, with scheduled phone and video meetings during business hours.

Security Without Compromise

Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit, protected by multi-factor authentication, and governed by strict access controls. We follow SEC cybersecurity guidelines and conduct regular audits.

Continuous Improvement

We audit our AI systems, incorporate client feedback, and stay current with regulatory changes and financial research. Our goal is to get better for you every quarter.

How Our AI Advisory Works

Our advisory platform is powered by specialized AI agent teams, each trained in a distinct area of financial expertise. Rather than a single generalist, you have an entire team working on your behalf.

Financial Planning Team
Led by a CFP-standard AI, this team handles retirement planning, tax strategy, estate planning, education funding, and risk management.
Investment Management Team
Led by a CFA-standard AI, this team constructs and manages your portfolio using evidence-based strategies, low-cost funds, and disciplined rebalancing.
Compliance Team
Our AI Chief Compliance Officer reviews every recommendation before it reaches you, ensuring regulatory adherence and suitability.
Client Advisor
Your single point of contact who coordinates across all teams, answers your questions, and ensures a seamless experience.

Human Oversight & Governance

AI powers our efficiency. Humans ensure our accountability.

Chief Compliance Officer

A licensed human CCO oversees all compliance operations, reviews escalated matters, conducts annual compliance reviews, and manages regulatory examinations.

Escalation Protocols

Complex situations, client complaints, and unusual requests are automatically escalated to human review. You can also request human review of any recommendation at any time.

Regular Audits

Quarterly audits of AI recommendations, annual penetration testing, and external compliance reviews ensure our systems perform to the highest standards.

Regulatory Registration

We are registered with the SEC as an investment advisor and operate under the same fiduciary regulations as every traditional RIA firm.

Our Fiduciary Commitment

As a registered investment advisor, we are legally bound to act in your best interest. This isn’t a marketing claim—it’s a regulatory obligation we take seriously in every recommendation, every trade, and every interaction.
