Legal Disclosures

Transparency is a core value. Below you’ll find our regulatory filings, privacy policy, terms of service, and other important legal information.

Form ADV Part 2A — Firm Brochure

Form ADV Part 2A is the primary disclosure document for registered investment advisors. It provides clients and prospective clients with information about our firm, services, fees, and business practices.

Advisory Business

Meridian Wealth Advisors, LLC is a registered investment advisor providing financial planning and investment management services. Advisory services are delivered through AI-powered systems with human oversight by a licensed Chief Compliance Officer.

Fees and Compensation

We charge asset-based fees ranging from 0.45% to 0.75% annually, depending on the service tier. Planning-only fees range from $1,200 to $3,500. We do not receive commissions, revenue sharing, or any other form of compensation beyond our stated advisory fees. See our Pricing page for full details.

Types of Clients

We provide services to individuals, high-net-worth individuals, and families. Our Essential tier has a minimum quarterly fee of $500 ($2,000/year).

Methods of Analysis and Investment Strategies

We use evidence-based investing strategies centered on Modern Portfolio Theory. Portfolios are constructed using low-cost index funds and ETFs, with asset allocation tailored to each client’s risk tolerance, time horizon, and financial goals. We employ tax-loss harvesting, automatic rebalancing, and asset location optimization.

Risk of Loss

Investing in securities involves risk of loss that clients should be prepared to bear. Past performance is not indicative of future results. While diversification can reduce risk, it does not eliminate it. Market conditions, economic factors, and other variables can cause the value of investments to decline.

Disciplinary Information

Meridian Wealth Advisors, LLC has no disciplinary history and has not been the subject of any legal or regulatory proceedings.

Form CRS — Client Relationship Summary

Introduction

Meridian Wealth Advisors, LLC is registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) as an investment adviser. Free and simple tools are available to research firms and financial professionals at investor.gov/CRS, which also provides educational materials about broker-dealers, investment advisers, and investing.

What investment services and advice can you provide me?

We offer financial planning and investment management services to individuals and families. Our services include comprehensive financial planning (retirement, tax strategy, estate planning, education funding, risk management), portfolio construction and management, tax-loss harvesting, and ongoing monitoring. Services are delivered through AI-powered advisory teams with human compliance oversight.

What fees will I pay?

We charge an annual advisory fee based on your assets under management, ranging from 0.45% to 0.75%. Planning-only services are available for a flat fee of $1,200–$3,500. You will also pay fees and costs related to the underlying investments in your portfolio (fund expense ratios). Custodians may charge transaction fees for certain trades.

What are your legal obligations to me?

When we act as your investment adviser, we must act in your best interest and not put our interest ahead of yours. We are held to a fiduciary standard, which means we must eliminate or disclose any conflicts of interest that might influence our advice.

How might your conflicts of interest affect me?

As a fee-only advisor, our conflicts are minimal. We do not receive commissions, sell proprietary products, or participate in revenue sharing. Our sole compensation is the advisory fee you pay. We disclose all potential conflicts in our Form ADV Part 2A.

Do you or your financial professionals have legal or disciplinary history?

No. Visit investor.gov/CRS for a free and simple search tool to research our firm and our financial professionals.

Privacy Policy

Meridian Wealth Advisors, LLC (“we,” “our,” or “the Firm”) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients’ personal information. This notice describes our privacy practices in accordance with Regulation S-P.

Information We Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, address, date of birth, Social Security number)
  • Financial information (income, assets, debts, tax returns, account balances)
  • Employment and income information
  • Investment objectives, risk tolerance, and financial goals
  • Communications (chat transcripts, emails, meeting recordings, phone calls)
  • Website and portal usage data

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide financial planning and investment management services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To maintain and improve our AI advisory systems
  • To communicate with you about your account and services
  • To prevent fraud and protect our systems

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Custodians (Fidelity, Charles Schwab) to execute trades and maintain your accounts
  • Service providers who assist us in delivering our services (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Regulatory authorities as required by law (SEC, state regulators)

Data Security

  • All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Multi-factor authentication required for all accounts
  • Role-based access controls
  • Regular penetration testing and security audits
  • Incident response plan with notification procedures

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at privacy@meridianwealthadvisors.com. Note that we are required to retain certain records for compliance purposes as mandated by the SEC.

Business Continuity Plan

Meridian Wealth Advisors maintains a comprehensive Business Continuity Plan (BCP) designed to ensure continuous operation of critical business functions in the event of a disruption.

Key Elements

  • Redundant cloud infrastructure with failover capabilities
  • Daily encrypted backups with disaster recovery (RTO: 4 hours, RPO: 1 hour)
  • 99.9% uptime SLA with hosting provider
  • 24/7 system monitoring and automated alerts
  • Manual processes for critical operations during extended outages
  • Annual BCP testing and updates

In the event of a significant disruption, clients will be notified via email and through our website. Your assets remain safe with our qualified custodians regardless of any disruption to our advisory operations.

Custodian & Account Protection

Client assets are held by qualified custodians—Fidelity Investments and Charles Schwab—not by Meridian Wealth Advisors. We never take direct custody of client funds or securities.

SIPC Coverage

Accounts held at our custodians are protected by the Securities Investor Protection Corporation (SIPC) up to $500,000 per account (including $250,000 for cash claims). SIPC protection covers the custody function of a broker-dealer—it does not protect against market losses.

Additional Insurance

Both Fidelity and Charles Schwab carry additional insurance coverage beyond SIPC limits. Please refer to your custodian’s website for specific coverage details.

FINRA

Our custodians are members of the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA). You can verify their registration and review any disclosures at brokercheck.finra.org.
