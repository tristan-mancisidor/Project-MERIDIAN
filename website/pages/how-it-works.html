Your Path to Financial Confidence

A simple, guided process that takes you from your first conversation to ongoing wealth management—powered by AI, overseen by professionals.

Four Steps to a Better Financial Future

1

Discovery

We start with a free consultation to understand your financial situation, goals, and what matters most to you. You’ll share details about your income, assets, debts, and aspirations so our AI advisors can build a complete picture of your financial life.

2

Plan

Our CFP-standard AI planning team creates a comprehensive, personalized financial plan covering retirement, tax strategy, estate planning, education funding, and risk management. Every recommendation is reviewed by our compliance team for suitability.

3

Implement

Once you approve your plan, our CFA-standard investment team builds your portfolio using low-cost index funds and ETFs. Accounts are opened with our qualified custodians (Fidelity or Charles Schwab), and your strategy is put into action.

4

Monitor & Optimize

Your portfolio is continuously monitored for rebalancing opportunities and tax-loss harvesting. Your financial plan is updated as your life changes. You have 24/7 access to your AI advisor team through chat, with scheduled video meetings available during business hours.

Technology & Security

Bank-Level Encryption

All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Your financial information is protected by the same standards used by major financial institutions.

Two-Factor Authentication

Every account is protected by 2FA, with biometric login available on mobile. Login notifications and session timeouts add additional layers of protection.

Compliance Monitoring

Our AI Chief Compliance Officer reviews every recommendation before it reaches you. Quarterly audits, annual penetration testing, and external compliance reviews ensure the highest standards.
