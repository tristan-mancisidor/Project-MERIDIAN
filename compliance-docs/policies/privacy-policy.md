## Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Meridian Wealth Advisors, LLC ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect the nonpublic personal information of our clients and prospective clients.

## Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of nonpublic personal information:

**Information you provide directly:**
- Name, address, date of birth, Social Security number
- Employment and income information
- Financial account information, assets, and liabilities
- Investment goals, risk tolerance, and preferences
- Tax information and filing status
- Beneficiary and estate planning details

**Information from your accounts and transactions:**
- Account balances and positions
- Transaction history
- Portfolio performance data
- Fee and billing records

**Information from third parties:**
- Account data from custodians (Fidelity, Schwab)
- Credit and identity verification services
- Financial planning software (eMoney Advisor)

**Information collected automatically:**
- Client portal usage data (login activity, pages viewed)
- Communication logs (chat, email, phone, video)
- Device and browser information for security purposes

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide financial planning and investment advisory services
- Manage and monitor your investment portfolio
- Generate personalized financial recommendations
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
- Communicate with you about your accounts and services
- Improve our AI advisory systems and service quality
- Detect and prevent fraud and unauthorized access

## AI-Specific Privacy Practices

Our AI advisory systems process your personal information to generate personalized financial advice. Specifically:

- Your financial data is used to train and calibrate recommendations for your account only
- Your data is never used to train general AI models or shared with AI training datasets
- AI-generated recommendations are logged for compliance and quality assurance
- You may request a human review of any AI-generated recommendation

## Information We Share

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

**Service providers (as necessary to deliver our services):**
- Custodians (Fidelity, Schwab) - for account management and trading
- Financial planning software providers - for plan generation
- CRM system - for relationship management
- Transcription services - for meeting notes (with your consent)

**Legal and regulatory:**
- As required by law, regulation, or legal process
- In response to requests from regulatory authorities (SEC, state regulators)
- To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

**With your consent:**
- To third-party professionals you designate (CPAs, attorneys)
- For any other purpose with your explicit written consent

## Information Security

We protect your information through:

- **Encryption**: All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- **Access controls**: Role-based access limited to need-to-know basis
- **Authentication**: Multi-factor authentication required for all account access
- **Monitoring**: Continuous security monitoring and anomaly detection
- **Testing**: Quarterly penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
- **Incident response**: Documented plan with notification procedures
- **Vendor management**: Security requirements for all third-party providers

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for the duration of our advisory relationship plus five (5) years, as required by SEC Rule 204-2. Certain records may be retained longer as required by law. Upon request, we will inform you of the specific retention period for your data.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Opt out of certain information sharing (where applicable)
- Receive this privacy notice annually
- Request information about our privacy practices
- File a complaint if you believe your privacy rights have been violated

## State-Specific Rights

Residents of certain states (including California under CCPA) may have additional rights regarding their personal information. Contact us for state-specific privacy information.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or through your client portal. The effective date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.
