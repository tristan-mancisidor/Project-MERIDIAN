## Introduction

Meridian Wealth Advisors, LLC is registered with the [SEC / State of ___] as an investment adviser. Our advisory services are provided by AI-powered systems with human oversight. Brokerage and investment advisory services and fees differ, and it is important for you to understand these differences. Free and simple tools are available to research firms and financial professionals at Investor.gov/CRS, which also provides educational materials about broker-dealers, investment advisers, and investing.

## What investment services and advice can you provide me?

**Services offered**: We offer investment advisory services including:
- Comprehensive financial planning
- Discretionary investment management
- Targeted financial planning (single-topic)

**Monitoring**: Your portfolio is monitored continuously by our AI systems with automated rebalancing.

**Investment authority**: We manage accounts on a discretionary basis, meaning we make investment decisions without contacting you in advance for each trade.

**Account minimums**: $50,000 minimum for managed accounts. $1,200 minimum for planning-only services.

**AI Disclosure**: Our advisory services are delivered by AI-powered agents. A description of our AI advisory model, human oversight procedures, and your right to request human review is available in our Form ADV Part 2A.

> **Conversation Starter**: "Given my financial situation, should I choose an investment advisory service? Why or why not? How will you choose investments to recommend to me? What is your relevant experience, including your licenses, education, and other qualifications? What do these qualifications mean?"

## What fees will I pay?

**Asset-based fees**: 0.45%-0.75% annually, billed quarterly in arrears based on account value.

**Planning fees**: $1,200-$3,500 depending on scope.

**Other costs**: You will pay the expense ratios of ETFs/funds in your portfolio (typically 0.03%-0.12%). Custodians may charge transaction fees for certain trades.

**Fee impact**: You will pay fees and costs whether you make or lose money on your investments. Fees and costs will reduce any amount of money you make on your investments over time. Please make sure you understand what fees and costs you are paying.

> **Conversation Starter**: "Help me understand how these fees and costs might affect my investments. If I give you $100,000 to invest, how much will go to fees and costs, and how much will be invested for me?"

## What are your legal obligations to me when acting as my investment adviser? How else does your firm make money and what conflicts of interest do you have?

When we act as your investment adviser, we have to act in your best interest and not put our interest ahead of yours. At the same time, the way we make money creates some conflicts with your interests. You should understand and ask us about these conflicts because they can affect the investment advice we provide you.

**Conflicts**: Our only source of revenue is the advisory fees described above. We do not receive commissions, revenue sharing, or compensation from any third party for recommending specific investments.

> **Conversation Starter**: "How might your conflicts of interest affect me, and how will you address them?"

## How do your financial professionals make money?

Our AI advisory agents are not compensated individually. The firm earns revenue solely from client advisory fees.

## Do you or your financial professionals have legal or disciplinary history?

No. Visit Investor.gov/CRS for a free and simple search tool to research us and our financial professionals.

> **Conversation Starter**: "As a financial professional, do you have any disciplinary history? For what type of conduct?"
